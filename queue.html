Commission Queue

Commissions use codenames for the sake of privacy. The commissioners know which codename belongs to them. Codenames are random dictionary words, there's no intentional relation to the content of the commissions.

The front of the queue is at the top. Later tasks are added at the bottom. I don't necessarily work in strictly that order. Smaller jobs may get squeezed into smaller time slots. Pieces of the same type may block each other, e.g. I'm unlikely to start a story while still working on one, but may begin a drawing instead.
